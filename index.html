About

I am a Lecturer and Researcher at Universidad Rey Juan Carlos. My research focuses on sport communication, social media, media coverage and polarization, with particular interest in how public controversies circulate across digital platforms and news media.

Research

My work connects sport, media studies and digital communication, with attention to polarization, discourse and public controversies.

01
Sport communication
Media narratives, sports journalism and public controversies around sport.
02
Social media and public debate
User interaction, sentiment, emotions and discourse on digital platforms.
03
Media and polarization
How ideological and discursive polarization emerges in media coverage and audience responses.
04
Gender equality in sport
Communication about equal pay, feminism and gender debates in sport contexts.
